# Consent Agreement — TTRPG Open Dataset

This is the full text of the consent agreement presented to users via the Bot's consent prompt.

---

## Recording Consent

By clicking **Accept**, you agree to the following:

1. **Your audio will be recorded** as a separate per-speaker track during this Discord voice session.

2. **Your audio will be transcribed** using automatic speech recognition and reviewed for personal information.

3. **Your audio and transcript will be released** as part of a public dataset under the **Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International License (CC BY-SA 4.0)**.

4. **Your Discord identity will be pseudonymized** in the public dataset. Your Discord username and user ID will not appear in the published data.

5. **You may withdraw consent** at any time before your audio is included in a published dataset release by contacting the project maintainer.

6. **You are at least 18 years old**, or have parental/guardian consent to participate.

## What "CC BY-SA 4.0" Means

- Anyone may use, share, and adapt the dataset, including for commercial purposes
- Anyone who redistributes the data must give credit and use the same license
- This license is irrevocable once the dataset is published — you cannot revoke access to copies already distributed

## Your Options

- **Accept**: Your audio will be recorded and included in the dataset
- **Decline Audio**: You remain in the voice channel, but your audio is not recorded
- **Decline**: You decline participation entirely
